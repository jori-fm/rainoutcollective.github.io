About Us

RAINOUT collective. is an independent label and creative group focused on releasing music and visuals with a DIY spirit. We value experimentation, collaboration, and giving artists space to build their sound.

This site hosts our latest releases and artist profiles. Stick around — more is coming.

What We Do

  • Curate and release digital singles, EPs, and LPs.
  • Offer light-weight support on artwork, rollout, and distribution.
  • Connect artists for features, remixes, and cross-project ideas.

FAQ

How do I join or collaborate?

We usually reach out directly when something fits, but we do read demo emails. If you have a concept that aligns, send a short note and one or two links — keep it simple. You can contact us at rainout.co@aol.com

Do you sell physical releases?

Not yet, however we plan to later this year.

Can I use your visuals?

Please ask first; most artwork is licensed for the specific release.
