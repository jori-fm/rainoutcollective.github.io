Biography

Shinrei is an experimental artist from Detroit, Michigan, creating music in industrial, lo-fi, and dreampunk styles. Influenced by artists like Yves Tumor, Flying Lotus, Jane Remover, and JPEGMAFIA, he’s known for uniquely mixing using his inspiration.

He began making music at 13 using BandLab, later moving to FL Studio in 2024. In July 2025, Shinrei joined the RAINOUT collective, releasing music solo and collaborating with other members.
