Biography

Smooch is an artist who’s never been comfortable fitting into the norm. Constantly evolving, they experiment with every project—changing, adding, or removing elements to push their sound forward. From early tracks like seawrld to their recent standout Yes, or Yes., smooch. continues to grow with each release.

They made their debut under RAINOUT with the EP A witness, A watcher in May 2025. Earlier work like A day down the park and guitar_4 appeared under ROBALLS INC. Their first full-length album is expected in 2026.
