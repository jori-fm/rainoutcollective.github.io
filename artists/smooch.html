Biography

smooch. is a New Jersey-based producer making genre-blending music in the electronic, dnb, jungle, and digicore space. Since joining RAINOUT in late 2024, he’s released experimental tracks and EPs that highlight his raw and nostalgic sound.

He made his debut under RAINOUT with the EP A witness, A watcher in May 2025. Earlier work like A day down the park and guitar_4 appeared under ROBALLS INC.. His first full-length album is expected in 2026.
